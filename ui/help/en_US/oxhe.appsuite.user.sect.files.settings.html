Files Settings

How to use the files settings:

  1. Click the System menu icon at the right side of the menu bar. Click on Settings in the menu.

  2. Click on Files in the sidebar .

  3. Change the settings.

The following settings are available.

  • Default view

  • Audio enabled

  • Video enabled

Default view. Defines the default view for the files.

Audio enabled. Enables or disables the audio player.

Video enabled. Enables or disables the video player.
