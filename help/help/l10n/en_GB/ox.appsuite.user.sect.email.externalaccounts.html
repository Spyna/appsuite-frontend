E-Mail

5.8. E-Mail Accounts

If you use external E-Mail accounts e.g., Google Mail, you can access those E-Mail accounts provided you have set up the external E-Mail accounts in the settings. Each external E-Mail account will receive its own E-Mail folder.

  • First you have to set up or edit external E-Mail accounts.

  • You can then use the external E-Mail accounts.

You can change the folders of your primary E-Mail account by assigning other folders to the default folders.
