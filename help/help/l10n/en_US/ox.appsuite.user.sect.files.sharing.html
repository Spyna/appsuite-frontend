9.6. Sharing

Sharing objects allows to work with other users or external partners. There are the following options:

  • Share single files or folders for other users or external partners with read or edit access.

  • Access single files or folders shared with you by other users.

  • Get an overview of your shared objects or change single shared objects by managing them on an overview page.
