5.9.1. Sharing Email Messages

You can share your E-Mail messages with internal users. Depending on the requirements, different methods exist.

  • To make email messages available to internal users, proceed as follows:

    • Create a new personal folder to which you will copy or move the required email messages.

    • Share this folder.

    • The users have to subscribe to your shared E-Mail folder in order to have access to the E-Mail messages.

  • If another user shared an E-Mail folder with you, you have to subscribe to the shared E-Mail folder in order to have access to the E-Mail messages.
