6.9. Address Book Settings

How to use the address book settings:

  1. Click the System menu icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click the Settings menu item.

  2. If Address Book is not displayed in the sidebar, enable Advanced Settings at the bottom of the sidebar.

    Click on Address Book in the sidebar.

  3. Change the settings.

The following settings are available.

  • display of names

  • My contact data

display of names Specifies the order in which a contact's first name and surname are displayed in the address book.

My contact data Opens a page where you can change your personal contact data.
