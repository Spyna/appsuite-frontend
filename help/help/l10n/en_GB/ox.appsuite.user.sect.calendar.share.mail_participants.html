7.8.2. Sending an Email to participants

You can send an email to all appointment participants.

How to send an Email to all appointment participants:

  1. Select an appointment from the list or in a calendar view.

  2. Click on Send e-mail to all participants in the tool bar.

  3. Fill in the details in order to Send a new E-Mail.
