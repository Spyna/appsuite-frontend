Moving E-Mails

You can move a single E-Mail or a complete E-Mail thread to another folder.

How to move an E-Mail:

Prerequisite: You have the permission to create objects in the target folder.

  1. Select an E-Mail in the sidebar.

  2. Click on More in the display area. Select Move from the menu.

  3. Select a folder in the Move window. Click on the Move button.

Tip: You can also move multiple E-Mails at once or all E-Mails of an E-Mail thread.
