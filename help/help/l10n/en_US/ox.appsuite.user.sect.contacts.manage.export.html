6.8.6. Exporting contacts

You can export contacts or distribution lists to those formats:

  • vCard. You can e.g. use this format to exchange contacts with other E-Mail applications.

  • CSV You can use this format to export contacts to spreadsheet formats.

How to export contacts:

  1. Select a single contact or distribution list, multiple contacts or distribution lists from the list.

  2. Click the More actions iconin the toolbar. Click the Export menu item. The Export selected dialog window opens.

  3. Select a format.

    To export selected distribution lists, enable With distribution lists.

  4. Click on Export. Complete the entries.
