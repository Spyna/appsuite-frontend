9.5.3. Showing the link to a file

You can show the link to a file that is saved in the Drive app.

How to show the link to a file:

  • Click on a file in the detail view. In the pop-up, click on More. Click on Show internal link in the menu.

    You can also select one or several files. Click on More in the toolbar. Click on Show internal link in the menu.
