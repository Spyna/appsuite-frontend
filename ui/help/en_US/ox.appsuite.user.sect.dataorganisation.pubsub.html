Publish&Subscribe

Publish and subscribe helps you using social network data or sharing specific data with external partners. The following possibilities exist:

  • Import contacts from social networks like LinkedIn or Facebook to a contacts folder or import appointments from your Google calendar to a calendar folder by subscribing those data.

  • Share the data of a contacts or documents folder with other users and external partners by publishing the contacts or documents folder.
