Organizing Contacts

Some of the techniques for organizing contacts require own contact folders having been set up by you. Information about creating folders can be found in Folders.

The following possibilities exist:

  • Sending E-Mails from within an address book

  • Inviting contacts to an appointment

  • Editing contacts

  • Moving contacts

  • Copying contacts

  • Deleting contacts
