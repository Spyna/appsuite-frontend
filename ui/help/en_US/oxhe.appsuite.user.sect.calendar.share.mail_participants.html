Sending an E-Mail to participants

You can send an E-Mail to all participants of an appointment.

How to send an E-Mail to all participants of an appointment:

  1. Depending on the view selected, use one of the following methods:

    • In the views Day, Work week, Week or Month, click on an appointment. In the pop-up, click on Send mail to all participants.

    • In the List view, select an appointment in the sidebar. Click on Send mail to all participants in the display area.

  2. Complete the details for Sending the E-Mail.
