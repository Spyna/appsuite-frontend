5.4.4. Automatically forwarding email messages

You can let email messages be automatically forwarded to another address.

How to automatically forward email messages:

  1. Click the System menu icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click the Settings menu item.

  2. Select the entry E-Mail in the side bar. Click on Auto Forward.

  3. Enter the email address to which you want to forward the messages.

  4. Enable Enabled.

  5. In order to keep a copy of the E-Mail, enable Keep a copy of the message.
