6.8.7. Inviting contacts to XING

You can invite a contact to XING if this contact does not have an XING account yet.

How to invite a contact to XING:

  1. Select a contact from the list.

  2. Click the More actions iconin the tool bar. Click the menu entry Invite to XING.
