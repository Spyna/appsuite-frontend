9.9. Drive Settings

How to use the Drive settings:

  1. Click the System menu icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click the Settings menu item.

  2. If Drive is not displayed in the side bar, enable Advanced Settings at the bottom of the side bar.

    Click on Drive in the side bar.

  3. Change the settings.

The following settings are available:

  • Show hidden files and folders

Show hidden files and folders Defines whether hidden files and folders are displayed. Such files and folders might be created by the local Drive clients.
