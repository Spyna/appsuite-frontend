Creating Office Documents

You can create Office documents with formatted text, tables, and images.

How to create an Office document:

  1. Open a folder in the folder tree.

    Note: Open a folder for which you have the permission to create objects.

  2. Click the New icon in the toolbar. Click on New office document.

  3. Enter the text or copy text from the clipboard.

  4. Enter a title. Click on Save.
