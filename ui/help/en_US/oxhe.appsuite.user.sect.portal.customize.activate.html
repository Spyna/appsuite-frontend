Enabling or disabling tiles

You can set a tile's visibility by enabling or disabling the tile.

How to enable or disable a tile:

  1. Click on Customize this page on the right side below the menu bar.

  2. In order to enable a tile, click on Enable next to the tile.

    In order to disable a tile, click on Disable next to the tile.
