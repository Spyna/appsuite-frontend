6.6.2. Subscribing to E-Mail folders

In order to see E-Mail folders shared by other users, you have to subscribe to those folders.

How to subscribe to shared E-Mail folders:

  1. Open the folder tree.

  2. Click on the Add button below the folder tree. Click on Subscribe IMAP folder.

  3. In the Subscribe IMAP folders window activate the checkboxes of the folders that you want to subscribe to. Click on Save.
