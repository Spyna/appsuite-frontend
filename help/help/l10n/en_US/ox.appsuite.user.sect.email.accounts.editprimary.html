5.9.1. Editing the primary E-Mail account

There are the following options:

  • You can change the name of your primary E-Mail account. This name is displayed in the E-Mail settings.

  • You can change the folders of your primary E-Mail account by assigning other folders to a standard folder.

How to edit the primary E-Mail account:

  1. Click the System menu icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click the Settings menu item.

  2. In the sidebar, click on Accounts.

  3. Click on Edit next to E-Mail in the display area. The Edit mail account window displays the current settings.

  4. To change the account name, click into the input field next to Account name.

  5. To select other folders, browse down to Standard folders.

    Click on Select next to a folder. Select another folder.

  6. Click on Save at the bottom of the window.
