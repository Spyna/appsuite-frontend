Copying E-Mails

You can copy an individual E-Mail or a complete E-Mail thread to another folder.

How to copy an E-Mail:

Prerequisite: You have permission to create objects in the target folder.

  1. Select an E-Mail in the sidebar.

  2. Click on Copy in the display area. Select Copy from the menu.

  3. Select a folder in the Copy window. Click on Copy.

Tip: You can also copy multiple E-Mails at once or all the E-Mails in an E-Mail thread.
