12.2. Folders

Folders help you

  • keep an overview of your objects

  • share information with other users and external partners

  • search for certain information and quickly find the information again

Learn more about folders and how to use them:

  • the folder types

  • navigate within the folder structure

  • hide certain folders

  • create, rename,delete, and move folders or subfolders.
