Address Book Settings

How to use the address book settings:

  1. Click the System menu icon in the right side of the menu bar. Click on Settings in the menu.

  2. Click on Address book in the sidebar.

  3. Change the settings.

The following options are available.

  • Show contacts from administrator group

Show contacts from administrator group Specifies whether contacts from the adminstrator group are displayed.
