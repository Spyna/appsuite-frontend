7.9.2. Sending an E-mail to participants

You can send an e-mail to all appointment participants.

How to send an Email to all appointment participants:

  1. Depending on the view selected, use one of the following methods:

    • In a calendar view, click on an appointment with multiple participants. In the pop-up, click the More actions icon. Click on Send mail to all participants in the menu.

    • In the list view, double-click on an appointment with multiple participants. The appointment will be displayed in a window. Click the More actions icon. Click on Send mail to all participants in the menu.

  2. Fill in the details in order to Send a new E-Mail.
