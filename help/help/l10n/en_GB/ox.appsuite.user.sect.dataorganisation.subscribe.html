10.4. Managing Subscriptions

In this section you will learn how to manage the subscriptions that you set up:

  • appointments from your Google calendar or from calendars that are publicly available on the Internet.

  • calendars that are publicly available on the Internet.

  • contacts from your social networks

The following options are available:

  • refresh subscribed data

    You can transfer changes that have been applied to subscribed data to the groupware by refreshing the subscribed data.

  • Managing subscribed folders

    In order to keep the overview of your subscribed data, you can manage it on an overview page.
