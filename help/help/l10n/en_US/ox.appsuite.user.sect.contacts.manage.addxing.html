6.7.7. Adding contacts as Xing contacts

You can add a contact as Xing contact if you are not connected with this contact in Xing. The contact and you need to have a Xing account.

How to add a contact as Xing contact:

  1. Select a contact from the list.

  2. Click the More icon in the toolbar. Click on Add to Xing in the menu.
