12.2.1. Displaying buddy lists

In order to start a conversation, you can use the functions of the buddy list selected. The buddy list stays open until you close it again. Note: Buddy list and notification area overlap themselves.

How to open the buddy list:

  • Click the Buddy list icon on the right side of the menu bar.

    In order to close the buddy list, click the icon in the menu bar again.
