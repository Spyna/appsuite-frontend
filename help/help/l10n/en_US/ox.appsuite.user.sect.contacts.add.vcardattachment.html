6.4.2. Adding a contact from vCard

You can add a contact from a vCard attachment to an E-Mail. A vCard attachment per default has the name extension vcf.

How to add a contact from an E-Mail's vCard attachment:

  1. Select an E-Mail with a vCard attachment in the E-Mail application.

  2. Click on the attachment's name in the detail view. Click on Add to address book in the menu.
