7.4.11. Subscribing appointments from external calendars

You can import appointments from external calendars into calendar folders, e.g. from your Google calendar. To do so, subscribe the external calendar. The following applies:

  • The subscribed appointments are saved in a separate calendar in the groupware. Changes that you apply to this calendar are not transfered to the external calendar.

  • Changes that you apply to the external calendar can be transfered to the groupware by refreshing subscribed data.

How to subscribe appointments from your Google calendar:

  1. Click on Subscribe calendar in the folder tree. The Subscribe calendar window opens.

  2. Click the Google icon A new browser window opens. Grant access to your Google calendar.

    The appointments will be imported to a new calendar. As soon as the import has been completed, the Subscribe calendar window will be closed.

Tip: A calendar with subscribed appointments is marked with the Subscribed icon . If clicking the icon, the Subscriptions settings are opened.
