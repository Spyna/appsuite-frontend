6.8.1. Sending E-Mails from within an address book

You can send an E-Mail from within your address book to contacts or to a distribution list.

How to send an E-Mail from within an address book:

  1. Select a single contact, multiple contacts or distribution lists from the list.

  2. Click on Send mail in the toolbar.

  3. Fill in the details for sending a new E-Mail.
