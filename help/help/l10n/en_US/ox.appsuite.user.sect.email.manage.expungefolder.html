5.5.16. Cleaning up E-Mail folders

Depending on the settings, E-Mails that you deleted from within an E-Mail client like Apple Mail, MS Outlook or Mozilla Thunderbird, might be deleted only after having signed our from the client. In the meantime, those E-Mails will be shown as crossed out in the groupware. To delete those E-Mails clean up the E-Mail folder.

How to clean up an E-Mail folder:

  1. In the folder tree, select the folder that you want to clean up.

  2. Click the Folder-specific actions icon next to the foder name. Click on Clean up.
