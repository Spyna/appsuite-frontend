4.2.4. Adding a Portal widget for social networks

The following options are available:

  • In order to access information and functions of your social networks, you can add widgets for social networks.

  • If you do not have a XING account, you can use a widget to create a new XING account.

How to add a widget for accessing social networks:

  1. In the Portal app click on Add widget on the upper right side. Select one of the entries. The widget will be added.

  2. To enable the access, add your social network account by clicking on the respective button in the widget.

How to create an XING account by using your groupware data:

  1. In the Portal app click on Add widget on the upper right side. Select XING. The widget will be added.

  2. Click on Create a Xing account using the data stored here in the widget.

  3. Check the suggested data for creating the XING account. In order to create the account, click on Confirm.
