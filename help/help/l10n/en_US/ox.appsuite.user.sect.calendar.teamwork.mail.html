7.8.2. Sending an E-Mail to participants

You can send an E-Mail to all appointment participants.

How to send an E-Mail to all appointment participants:

  1. Depending on the view selected, use one of the following methods:

    • Click on an appointment in a calendar view. Click on Send mail to all participants in the pop-up.

    • Select an appointment in the list view. Click on Send mail to all participants in the display area.

  2. Fill in the details in order to Send a new E-Mail.
