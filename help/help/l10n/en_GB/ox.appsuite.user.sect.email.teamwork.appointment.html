5.8.3. Inviting all Email recipients to an appointment

If there are several email recipients, all recipients can be invited to a new appointment.

How to invite all email recipients to an appointment:

  1. Select an E-Mail.

  2. Click the Actions icon in the detail view.

    Select Invite to appointment from the menu.

  3. Complete the data for Creating an appointment.
