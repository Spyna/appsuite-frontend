Managing Subscriptions

10.4.1. refresh subscribed data

The following changes are applied when refreshing subscribed data:

  • new appointments or contacts

  • edited appointments or contacts

  • deleted appointments

How to refresh subscribed data:

  1. Click the Settings icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click the Settings menu item.

  2. In the sidebar, click on Subscriptions.

  3. Click on refresh next to a subscription in the display area.
