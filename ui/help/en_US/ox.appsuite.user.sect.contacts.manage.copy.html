Copying contacts

You can copy one contact or multiple contacts together to another folder.

How to copy a contact to another folder:

Prerequisite: You need to have the permission to create objects in the target folder.

  1. Select a contact in the sidebar.

  2. Click on More in the display area. Select Copy from the menu.

  3. Select a folder in the Copy window. Click on OK.
