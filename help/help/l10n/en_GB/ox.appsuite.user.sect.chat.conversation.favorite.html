10.2.4. Adding chats to favourites

You can add frequently used chats to Favourites. Favourites are displayed at the top of the chat list under Favourites.

How to add a chat to Favourites:

  1. Select a private or group chat or a public channel from the list.

  2. Click the More actions iconon the upper right side.

    Select Add to favourites.

To remove the chat from the favourites, click the More actions icon. Select Remove from favourites.
