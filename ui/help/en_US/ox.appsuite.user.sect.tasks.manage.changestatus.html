11.5.5. Changing tasks confirmations

You can cahnge your task confirmation at a later point of time.

How to change your task confirmation:

  1. Select a task in the sidebar.

  2. Click on More in the display area. Click on Change confirmation status in the menu.

  3. A window opens. Select a confirmation status. If required, enter a message.

  4. Click on Change status.
