5.6.9. Adding an email to the portal

You can add an E-Mail as widget to the Portal.

How to add an email to the portal:

  1. Select an E-Mail.

  2. Click the More actions iconin the tool bar. Click on Add to portal in the menu.

    You can also use the More actions iconin the detail view.

    Note: When having selected an E-Mail conversation, this function is only available in the detail view, not in the tool bar.
