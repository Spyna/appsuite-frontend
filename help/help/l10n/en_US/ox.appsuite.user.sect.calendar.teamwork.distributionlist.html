7.8.4. Creating a distribution list from the list of participants

You can create a distribution list from an appointment's list of participants.

You can create a distribution list from an appointment's list of participants.

  1. Depending on the view selected, use one of the following methods:

    • Click on an appointment in a calendar view. In the pop-up, click on Save as distribution list.

    • Select an appointment in the list view. Click on Save as distribution list in the display area.

  2. Complete the details for creating the distribution list.
