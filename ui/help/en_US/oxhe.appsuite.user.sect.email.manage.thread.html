Editing all E-Mails of an E-Mail thread

You can activate the following functions for all E-Mails of an E-Mail thread at once:

  • forwarding E-Mails

  • moving or copying E-Mails to another folder

  • marking E-Mails as read or unread

  • categorizing E-Mails with a label

  • deleting E-Mails

How to activate a function for all E-Mails of an E-Mail thread at once:

  1. Select the current E-Mail of the E-Mail thread in the sidebar. The current E-Mail is listed on top of the E-Mail thread.

  2. Click on Entire thread on top of the E-Mail in the display area. Select a menu entry
