3.6. Setting up Accounts for Social Networks

How to set up an account for accessing social networks:

  1. Click the System menu icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click on Settings in the menu.

  2. Click on Mail and Social Accounts in the sidebar.

  3. Click on Add in the display area. Select a social network from the menu e.g., Facebook. You will be directed to the social network page where you are asked for your credentials.

  4. Enter your credentials for the social network account. In order to finish the process, click on the respective button.
