4.2.3. Adding portal squares

You can extend the existing squares by adding new ones.

How to add new portal squares:

  1. Click in the Portal App on Add widget on the upper right side.

  2. Some squares require additional data. Enter the required values. Click on Save.

Tip: In the Files app you can add a file as portal square.

Tip: In the E-Mail app you can add an E-Mail as portal square.
