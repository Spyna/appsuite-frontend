9.5.1. Sending files as a link

You can send a link to a file to a user. You can send links to multiple files at once.

How to send a link to a file:

  1. Click on a file in the detail view. In the pop-up, click on More. Click on Send as internal link in the menu.

    You can also select one or several files. Click on More in the toolbar. Click on Send as internal link in the menu.

  2. In the Compose new E-Mail page, complete the details for sending the E-Mail.
