10.2.5. Closing a chat

To keep a clear overview of the chat list, you can close or re-open chats.

How to close a chat:

  1. Select a private or group chat or a public channel from the list.

  2. Click the More actions iconon the upper right side.

    Select Close chat.

The chat in the list and the messages in the chat field will be closed. Icons for creating a new chat will be displayed in the chat field.

How to open a closed chat:

  1. Click on History in the list. All chats will be displayed in the chat field.

  2. Click on Open next to a chat.
