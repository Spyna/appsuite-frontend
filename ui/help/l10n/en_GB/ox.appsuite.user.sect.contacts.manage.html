6.6. Organising Contacts

Some of the techniques for organising contacts require that contact folders have already been set up by you. Information about creating folders can be found in Folders.

The following options are available:

  • Sending Email Messages from within an address book

  • Inviting contacts to an appointment

  • Editing contacts

  • Moving contacts

  • Copying contacts

  • Sending contacts as vCard

  • Printing contacts

  • Deleting contacts

  • Editing multiple contacts at once
