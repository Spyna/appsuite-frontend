8.6.2. Sending an Email to participants

You can send an email to all appointment participants.

How to send an Email to all appointment participants:

  1. Depending on the view selected, use one of the following methods:

    • In the views Day, Work week, Week or Month, click on an appointment. In the pop-up, click on Send email to all participants.

    • In the List view, select an appointment in the side bar. Click on Send email to all participants in the display area.

  2. Complete the details for Sending the email.
