6.7.6. Sending contacts as vCard

You can send a contact or multiple contacts at once as vCard attachment to an E-Mail.

How to send a contact as vCard attachment:

  1. Select a contact from the list.

  2. Click the Actions icon in the toolbar. Click on Send as vCard in the menu.

  3. Fill in the details for sending a new E-Mail.
