6.5.3. Marking email messages as read or unread

Unread email messages will have an Unread icon in the side bar. In order to mark an email as read or unread, you can do the following:

  • mark an individual email or a complete email thread as read or unread.

  • mark all email messages in an email folder as unread

How to mark an email as unread:

  1. Select a read email in the side bar.

  2. Click on Mark unread in the display area.

In order to mark this email as read, click on Mark read.

Tip: You can also mark multiple email messages at once or all the email messages in an email thread as being read.

How to mark all email messages in a folder as read:

  1. Select an email folder in the folder tree.

  2. Click the Edit folder icon below the folder tree. Click on Mark all email messages as read.
