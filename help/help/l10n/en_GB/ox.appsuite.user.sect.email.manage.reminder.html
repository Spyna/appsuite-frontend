5.5.8. Enabling an email reminder

You can enable a reminder for an email. This function creates a task and reminds you of the due date.

How to activate the email reminder:

  1. Select an E-Mail.

  2. Click on More in the tool bar. Select Reminder from the menu.

  3. Complete the details in the Remind me window. Select a time from the Remind me drop-down field. Click on Create reminder.
