Components

Appointments tile. Displays your current appointments. You can do the following:

  • If clicking on the tile, a list of scheduled appointments is displayed.

  • If clicking on an appointment, a pop-up opens. The pop-up shows the appointment's data.

  • If clicking on a participant a further pop-up opens.

  • In order to close a pop-up, click on Close at the top right corner.

Inbox tile. Displays new E-Mails. You can do the following:

  • If clicking on the tile, a list of E-Mails is displayed.

  • If clicking on an E-Mail, a pop-up opens. The pop-up displays the E-Mail's contents.

  • To delete, reply to or forward the E-Mail, click on the respective button in the pop-up.

  • To close a pop-up, click on Close at the top right corner.

  • To compose a new E-Mail, click on the Compose new E-Mail list item.

Quota tile. Displays the current quota used on your account on the server.
